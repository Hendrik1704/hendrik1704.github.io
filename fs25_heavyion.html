Heavy-Ion Physics Journal Club (FS 25)

About the Journal Club

Welcome to the Heavy-Ion Physics Journal Club at Wayne State University for the Fall Semester 2025! This club provides an opportunity to discuss recent advances in heavy-ion collision phenomenology and related topics in nuclear and particle physics.

Schedule

The journal club meets weekly on Fridays from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM in Room 312, Physics Building, Wayne State University.
